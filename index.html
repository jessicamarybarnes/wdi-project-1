Connect the cocktails

Rules

Step 1: Grab a friend and click 'Start' to play the game

Step 2: Click on the white boxes on the table to place a cocktail there

Step 3: Let your friend place their cocktail in a chosen location

Step 4: Try to line up 4 of your cocktails consecutively before your opponent does. This can be vertical, horizontal or vertical!
